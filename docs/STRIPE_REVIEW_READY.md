## STRIPE REVIEW READY (TEST MODE ONLY)

This document prepares copy for Stripe review/onboarding **without enabling live payments yet**.

### Business description
BeTalent is an online talent entertainment platform for short-form video performances where creators share performances and audiences discover and support talent.

### Product description
Users can purchase in-app virtual Coins and use them to support creators on the BeTalent platform. Coins are virtual, have no cash value outside the platform, and are not exchangeable for real-world currency.

### Customer flow
- A user signs up or signs in.
- The user selects a Coin pack to purchase.
- Checkout is completed through Stripe.
- Coins are delivered to the user’s in-app balance.
- The user can use Coins to support creators in the app.

### Fulfillment
Digital fulfillment. Coins are delivered immediately to the user’s in-app wallet balance after successful payment confirmation.

### Refund summary
Coin purchases are generally final and non-refundable once delivered. Refunds may be issued only for duplicate transactions, technical errors, or unauthorized payments, handled through support review.
